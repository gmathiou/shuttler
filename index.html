Shuttler

iOS app for crowdsourcing shuttle buses arrival time

View project onGitHub

Description

A new way for live tracking of buses that do not offer any location monitoring infrastructure. Shuttler is based on crowdsourcing data provided by the users of the bus services. In its current edition, Shuttler is targeted to support Inria's employees getting to Rocquencourt (Paris) via the shuttle services.
